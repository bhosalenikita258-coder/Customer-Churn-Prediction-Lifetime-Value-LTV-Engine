### Abstract

Customer churn prediction helps businesses identify customers who are likely to discontinue their services. This project analyzes telecom customer data, performs preprocessing and exploratory data analysis, and builds a predictive machine learning model. The results can support customer retention strategies and improve overall business performance.

### Objectives

  - Understand customer behavior.
  - Analyze churn patterns.
  - Build a churn prediction model.
  - Evaluate model performance.
  - Provide business recommendations.

### Methodology
